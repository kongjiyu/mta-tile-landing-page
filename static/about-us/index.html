About MTA

Material knowledge, built into every solution.

MTA Tile Adhesive Specialist Sdn Bhd supplies high-performance tile installation and construction materials for residential, commercial, and industrial projects across Malaysia.

Company overview

A focused specialist for tiling and construction needs.

MTA Tile Adhesive Specialist Sdn Bhd is a distributor of the MTA brand and a provider of tile adhesive solutions. Since 2021, the company has focused on dependable building materials and practical product support.
