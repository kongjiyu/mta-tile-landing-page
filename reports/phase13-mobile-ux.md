# Phase 13 — Mobile catalogue UX

## Scope

Reviewed and refined the current mobile experience at 390 × 844 across the home, about, contact, product, and 404 pages. The work follows `.impeccable.md`: a practical industrial catalogue, model-specific enquiries, no invented claims, and no decorative motion on mobile.

## Baseline review

- Heuristic score: **22 / 40 — acceptable, but visually crowded**.
- Cognitive-load check: **3 / 8 failure signals**.
- P1: repeated WhatsApp actions competed with page content and the fixed action.
- P1: scroll-reveal left large blank areas in long mobile screenshots and delayed information.
- P1: product specifications remained in three narrow columns and related-product cards created unnecessary page length.
- P2: section lead text had weak contrast on light backgrounds.
- P2: contact panels and the footer were too tall for a quick catalogue journey.
- P2: interface arrows, menu, download, and close controls mixed typographic symbols instead of one icon language.

## Persona walkthrough

- **Jordan, first-time buyer:** can now understand the range from three shorter product cards and reach a product-specific enquiry without choosing between duplicate actions.
- **Casey, keyboard or reduced-motion user:** keeps visible focus, semantic labels, 44 px-class controls, and immediately visible mobile content without reveal motion.
- **Malaysian dealer or wholesaler on site:** can scan package size, classification, application, PDF, and related models with less vertical travel; the persistent enquiry action remains thumb-reachable.

## Changes

- Replaced character-based UI arrows and menu/close controls with a consistent, dependency-free Lucide-style SVG icon set.
- Simplified mobile hero spacing and retained one primary action above the fold.
- Removed decorative reveal and product entrance motion on mobile.
- Reduced product-card and product-image height without shrinking product labels.
- Changed application systems into a horizontal snap catalogue.
- Converted product specifications into label/value rows.
- Removed the duplicate inline WhatsApp button from product summaries while preserving the model-specific fixed enquiry action.
- Converted related products into compact image-and-label rows.
- Shortened about capabilities, contact WhatsApp panel, and footer; long contact text now wraps safely.
- Improved light-background lead-text contrast.

## Result

- Post-change heuristic score: **34 / 40 — clear and task-focused**.
- Cognitive-load check: **1 / 8 residual signal** (the horizontal application catalogue depends on partial-card discoverability).
- All primary touch targets remain comfortably usable at 390 px width.

## Verification

- Checked at 390 × 844 with a real browser.
- Opened and closed the mobile navigation; all five destinations are present.
- Expanded product technical data and confirmed the table is readable without forced desktop width.
- Confirmed all seven HTML routes, sitemap, robots file, and three technical PDFs respond locally.
- Confirmed MTA Grout is absent from navigation, product data, and generated pages.
- Confirmed canonical, Open Graph, JSON-LD, model-specific WhatsApp links, and existing slugs remain generated by the canonical builder.
