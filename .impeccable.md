## Design Context

### Users
MTA's primary website audience is Malaysian tile-adhesive dealers and wholesalers. They arrive to identify the right product, verify packaging and technical suitability, download a catalogue, and contact sales quickly for a bulk enquiry.

### Brand Personality
Industrial, professional, and reliable. The site should feel grounded in real materials and technical competence rather than like a generic corporate template.

### Aesthetic Direction
An English-first, light industrial catalogue using warm cement neutrals, charcoal ink, MTA red, strong editorial typography, material texture, product packaging, and installation diagrams already owned by the company. Avoid glossy gradients, glassmorphism, generic card grids, decorative animations, and invented commercial claims.

### Design Principles
1. Products and their practical differences come before corporate storytelling.
2. Every product journey ends in a model-specific WhatsApp enquiry.
3. Technical content must be scannable without hiding essential facts.
4. Mobile is an adapted sales catalogue with thumb-reachable actions, not a shrunken desktop page.
5. Preserve verified source content, URLs, PDFs, and company details; never invent pricing, stock, MOQ, certification, or dealer policy.
